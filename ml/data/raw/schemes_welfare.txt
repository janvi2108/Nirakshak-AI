Government Welfare Schemes - Eligibility and Application Guide

PM AWAS YOJANA (PMAY) - Housing for All

Eligibility:
- Economically Weaker Section (EWS): Annual income up to Rs. 3 lakh
- Low Income Group (LIG): Annual income Rs. 3-6 lakh
- Middle Income Group (MIG-I): Annual income Rs. 6-12 lakh
- Middle Income Group (MIG-II): Annual income Rs. 12-18 lakh
- Must not own a pucca house anywhere in India
- Must not have availed housing subsidy from central government before

Required Documents: Aadhaar card, income certificate, bank account details, no-property affidavit

Apply at: pmaymis.gov.in or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)

---

OBC SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

Eligibility:
- Must belong to Other Backward Class (OBC) as per central/state list
- Annual family income: up to Rs. 1.5 lakh (pre-matric), up to Rs. 2.5 lakh (post-matric)
- Must be enrolled in a recognized institution

Required Documents: Caste certificate, income certificate, marksheets, Aadhaar, bank account

Apply at: scholarships.gov.in (National Scholarship Portal)

---

WIDOW PENSION SCHEME (Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme)

Eligibility:
- Widow aged 40-79 years (18-39 years in some states)
- Annual family income below poverty line
- BPL card holder preferred

Benefit: Rs. 300-500 per month (varies by state; state may add additional amount)
Apply at: District Social Welfare Office or state government portal

---

DISABILITY CERTIFICATE AND BENEFITS

Who qualifies: Person with 40% or more disability (physical, visual, hearing, intellectual)

Benefits available:
- 3% reservation in government jobs
- Concession in rail/bus travel
- Income tax deduction under Section 80U
- Assistive devices through ADIP scheme
- Scholarship for students with disability

How to get disability certificate:
- Apply at Chief Medical Officer (CMO) office
- Medical board evaluation required
- Certificate issued within 30 days

---

RATION CARD (National Food Security Act)

Categories:
- Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY): Poorest of poor families - 35 kg grain per month at Rs. 2/3 per kg
- Priority Household (PHH): 5 kg grain per person per month

How to apply: Visit tehsil/block office with Aadhaar cards of all family members, address proof, income proof. Apply online on state food department portal.

---

KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC)

For: Farmers requiring credit for agricultural needs

Benefit: Short-term credit at 7% interest rate (effective rate 4% with government subsidy for timely repayment)

Apply at: Any nationalized bank branch with land records, identity proof, and two passport photographs.

---

NATIONAL HEALTH MISSION (Ayushman Bharat - PMJAY)

Benefit: Health insurance cover of Rs. 5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary hospitalisation

Eligibility: Based on SECC 2011 data. Families listed in SECC database are automatically eligible.

Check eligibility: pmjay.gov.in or call 14555
