Birth Certificate Application Guide - Government of India

WHAT IS A BIRTH CERTIFICATE?
A birth certificate is an official document issued by the government that records the birth of a child. It is a vital document required for school admission, passport, Aadhaar card, and other government services.

ELIGIBILITY:
Any person born in India can apply for a birth certificate. The application can be made by the parents or legal guardian of the child. Adults can also apply for their own birth certificate if not previously issued.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
1. Hospital discharge summary or birth record from hospital
2. Parents' Aadhaar Card (both father and mother)
3. Parents' marriage certificate
4. Address proof (ration card, voter ID, electricity bill)
5. Affidavit if birth was not registered within 1 year
6. For delayed registration (after 1 year): Affidavit from Executive Magistrate

FEES:
- Registration within 21 days of birth: FREE
- Registration after 21 days but within 30 days: Rs. 2
- Registration after 30 days but within 1 year: Rs. 5
- Registration after 1 year: Rs. 10 plus court fee

HOW TO APPLY:
Step 1: Visit the Municipal Corporation office or Gram Panchayat office in your area.
Step 2: Fill the birth registration form (Form 1).
Step 3: Submit form with all required documents.
Step 4: The registrar will verify documents and issue a birth certificate.
Online: Apply at https://crsorgi.gov.in (Civil Registration System)

PROCESSING TIME:
- Standard processing: 7 working days
- Urgent processing (extra fee applicable): 3 working days
- Delayed registration (after 1 year): 15-30 working days due to inquiry process

COMMON REASONS FOR REJECTION:
1. Hospital records not matching application details
2. Parents' identity documents not submitted
3. Mismatch in date of birth across documents
4. Application submitted to wrong jurisdiction (must apply in the district where birth occurred)

CORRECTION OF ERRORS:
If your birth certificate has errors (wrong name, date of birth, parents' name), apply for correction at the same office with supporting documents. Correction fee is Rs. 50.

CONTACT:
For queries, contact your nearest Municipal Corporation office or call the helpline: 1800-180-1551
