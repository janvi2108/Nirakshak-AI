Income Certificate Application Guide

Purpose: Income certificates are required for various purposes including educational scholarships, fee concessions, and welfare scheme applications.

Eligibility: All citizens are eligible to apply regardless of income level. The certificate simply states the verified annual income of the applicant or family.

Required Documents:
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Salary slip or income proof (for salaried individuals)
3. Land records (for agricultural income)
4. Bank statements for the last 6 months
5. Self-employment proof (if applicable)

Application Process:
Apply through the state revenue department portal. Processing fee is Rs. 20. Field verification by a revenue official (patwari or equivalent) is typically required before issuance.

Processing Time:
Standard processing time is 6 working days. Rural applications requiring field visits may take up to 10 days.

Validity: Income certificates are typically valid for one financial year and need to be renewed annually for scheme applications.
