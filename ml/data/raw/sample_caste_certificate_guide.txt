Caste Certificate Application Guide

Eligibility: Any citizen belonging to SC, ST, or OBC category residing in the state for at least 10 years is eligible to apply for a caste certificate.

Required Documents:
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, or utility bill)
3. Caste certificate of father or other family member (if available)
4. Self-declaration affidavit
5. Two passport size photographs

Application Process:
Citizens can apply online through the state e-governance portal or visit their nearest tehsil/SDM office. The application fee is Rs. 30 for online applications.

Processing Time:
The standard processing time for caste certificates is 7 working days from the date of submission, provided all documents are in order. Applications requiring field verification may take up to 15 days.

Common Reasons for Rejection:
- Incomplete documentation
- Mismatch between applicant details and supporting documents
- Residence proof not matching application address
- Missing self-declaration affidavit

Appeal Process:
If an application is rejected, citizens can file an appeal with the District Magistrate within 30 days of rejection, along with supporting evidence.
